Looking for a place to travel?

Here are some photos of the prettiest and best places to travel to for social distancing.

TELOK MELANO

Sematan

Telok Melano is located at the tail-end of Sarawak. That what makes Telok Melano special as it is close to Indonesia country. But not only that, Telok Melano served a really beautiful view. To enter Telok Melano, it is free entrance, no charges like Pasir Pandak. It took 2 hours to reach Telok Melano from Kuching. It is good choices to choose Telok Melano as a place for vacation as it is far from hustle and bustle of the city. You can feel the calmness when visiting Telok Melano as enjoying the majestic view.

Visit

KUCHING WETLANDS PARK

Santubong

Kuching Wetlands National Park is located 15 km from Kuching city. Salak rivers is the main route for Kuching Wetlands National Park. The park offers people to enjoy the mangrove environment, and have the opportunity to see wildlife there. For example, proboscis monkeys, irrawaddy dolphin, shore birds and so on. The park offers a mesmerizing view of sunset as the pictures and videos provided on this sites. By enjoying the environment here, people can relief their stress and feels the relaxation.

Visit

PERMAI RAINFOREST RESORT

Santubong

Kem Permai also known as Permai Rainforest Resort which located at Santubong. It was just behind Damai Puri Resort. There is entrance fee to enter Kem Permai. There are lists of package that people can choose because Kem Permai offers various of activities. The package that my friends and I chose are going to Beach 1, Beach 2 and pool with rm10 entrance fee. Kem Permai is really recommended for the people who wants to have forest experience and enjoy the view of the sea.
